About us

Mylon Woodworks, established in 2022 in Antipolo City, Rizal, began as a small but rapidly growing woodworking business known for delivering custom-crafted pieces. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship and personalized service helped us quickly become a trusted choice for beautifully built wooden creations. As the demand for quality workmanship grew, we’ve expanded our services beyond custom carpentry and now offer general construction solutions.

Today, we cater to young professionals, new homeowners, and businesses seeking reliable construction services and beautifully crafted spaces. Dedicated to turning visions into functional and aesthetically pleasing environments, Mylon Woodworks continues to set the standard for quality, integrity, and craftsmanship in both woodworking and general construction.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional woodwork and construction solutions that elevate the way clients experience construction services. At Mylon Woodworks, we are committed to upholding transparency, integrity, and consistent excellence. We ensure our clients experience dependable service that eliminates worries of misinformation and dishonesty. Through quality craftsmanship, clear communication, and personalized service, we aim to earn our clients’ trust and exceed expectations at every stage of each project.

Our Vision

To become a trusted leader in woodworking and general construction, known for transforming homes and spaces through exceptional craftsmanship, innovation, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Process

Our process ensures that every project is transparent, well-planned, and executed with care, giving clients peace of mind while reflecting the principles that define Mylon Woodworks. Our communications do not stop when we finish the job. To ensure our clients get value for their money, we keep our lines open for any issues that they may encounter after the completion and turn-over of the project as part of our commitment to customer satisfaction and excellence.
